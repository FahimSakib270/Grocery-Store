Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our website, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You’ll need to enter your delivery details and select a payment method before confirming your order.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges vary based on your location and order total. Orders above a certain amount may qualify for free delivery. You can check the exact delivery fee at checkout.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes, if you receive a damaged or incorrect item, you can request a return or exchange within 24 hours of delivery. Please contact our support team with your order details and a photo of the product.

How can I track my order?

After placing an order, you’ll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also check your order status from the "My Orders" section on our website.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets (like bKash, Nagad), and cash on delivery (COD).
